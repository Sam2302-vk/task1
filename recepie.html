Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recepie has been in my family for genrations and never fails to delight and the recepie will long for genrations

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced

    • Instructions

    1.Preheat Oven to 375 deg F(190 deg celcius)
    2.In a bowl,mix the sugar and cinnamon.
    3.Toss the Apples in the sugar cinnamon mixture
